About Maddie Peterson

As a sophomore majoring in Sports Media and Communication and Advertising and Public Relations at the University of Nebraska-Lincoln, I’ve learned that there is a wide variety of opportunities within those industries. From public speaking, to coding, to creating campaigns, to making videos– I have been able to dip my toes into just about every area throughout my college experience thus far. I also have continued to seek out real-world experiences through jobs and internships that have helped me continuously learn and gain experience. I am currently an ambassador for UNL College of Journalism and Mass Communications, an intern for Paige Dimakos, and a student worker for the communications department within Nebraska Athletics.
